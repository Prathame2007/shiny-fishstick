FAQs

Will this void my warranty?

Bootloader unlocking may void warranty on many brands. Some allow relock on stock ROM. We’ll advise for your model.

Is my data safe?

Flashing can wipe data. We guide backups, but you are responsible for your data. We do not keep your data.

Is this legal?

We only work on devices you own and do not bypass FRP, DRM, or carrier locks. Proof of ownership is required.

Turnaround time?

Most jobs: 1–3 hours with an appointment. Complex/unbrick cases vary.
